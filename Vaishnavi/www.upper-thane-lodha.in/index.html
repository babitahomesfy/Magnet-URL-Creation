It was the largest BOT road project in India. The project involves increasing the number of lanes on the 99.5 km Vadape-Gonde (Mumbai-Nasik) section of the National Highway-6 lanes to 8 lanes.

It will connect Thane to Bhiwandi and Kalyan in the eastern suburbs.
